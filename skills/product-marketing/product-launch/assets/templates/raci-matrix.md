## RACI Key

**R = Responsible** (Does the work)
- Person(s) who do the actual work to complete the task
- Multiple people can be Responsible
- If you're Responsible, you're doing the hands-on work

**A = Accountable** (Owns the outcome)
- Person who is ultimately answerable for correct completion
- Only ONE person should be Accountable per task
- Makes final decisions, has veto power
- If you're Accountable, the buck stops with you

**C = Consulted** (Provides input)
- People whose opinions are sought
- Two-way communication (they provide expertise/feedback)
- Consulted before decisions are made

**I = Informed** (Kept in the loop)
- People who are kept up-to-date on progress
- One-way communication (they receive updates)
- Informed after decisions are made

## Escalation Path

**When issues arise:**

**Level 1: Team member tries to resolve**
- Timeline: Immediate to 2 hours

**Level 2: Escalate to Accountable person**
- Timeline: 2-4 hours
- Example: Bug during launch → PM → Eng Lead

**Level 3: Escalate to Launch Lead (PMM)**
- Timeline: 4-8 hours
- Example: Channel underperforming → Mktg → PMM

**Level 4: Escalate to Executive Sponsor**
- Timeline: >8 hours or critical issue
- Example: Site down, major crisis, need more resources

## Common RACI Mistakes to Avoid

**❌ Mistake 1: Multiple Accountable**
- Problem: Two people are "Accountable" for same task
- Result: No one owns it, task falls through cracks
- Fix: Only ONE person Accountable per task

**❌ Mistake 2: No Responsible**
- Problem: Someone is Accountable but no one is Responsible
- Result: Work doesn't get done
- Fix: Assign at least one Responsible person to do the work

**❌ Mistake 3: Everyone is Consulted**
- Problem: 8 people need to provide input on every decision
- Result: Decision paralysis, slow progress
- Fix: Only Consult people with specific expertise

**❌ Mistake 4: No one is Informed**
- Problem: Stakeholders surprised by launch decisions
- Result: Last-minute objections, delays
- Fix: Identify who needs to be kept in the loop

**❌ Mistake 5: RACI not updated**
- Problem: Created at kickoff, never revisited
- Result: Outdated, people don't follow it
- Fix: Review and update weekly during active launch
