# Launch Day Playbook

**Purpose:** Execute a flawless launch day with hour-by-hour tactics, war room coordination, and crisis protocols.

**When to use:** Day 61 of 90-day roadmap (Launch Day) and as preparation guide (Week 8).

**Output:** Successful launch execution with real-time monitoring, rapid response, and crisis management.

---

## Overview

Launch day is your highest-leverage 24 hours. Everything you've built for 60+ days comes down to flawless execution in a single day.

**Key principle:** Plan for perfection, prepare for chaos.

**The first 48 hours matter most:** Momentum from Day 1 carries through Week 1 and beyond.

## Crisis Protocols

### Types of Launch Day Crises

**1. Site Down / Major Bug**

**Response:**
- Product Monitor immediately notifies Commander
- Engineering team activates
- Communicate to users (Twitter, email if possible)
- Estimate time to fix
- Consider rollback if critical
- Post-mortem after resolution

**Communication template:**
"We're experiencing technical issues. Our team is on it. Updates in 15 minutes."

---

**2. Payment Processing Failure**

**Response:**
- Pause all paid acquisition immediately
- Put banner on site ("Free trial for now, payment issue being resolved")
- Fix payment provider issue
- Communicate to affected users
- Compensate affected users (extend trial, discount)

---

**3. Negative Sentiment / PR Crisis**

**Response:**
- Identify source and severity
- Commander decides response approach
- Respond quickly, authentically
- Address concerns directly
- Don't delete negative comments (makes it worse)
- Learn from feedback

**Example:** "You're right, [issue] is a problem. Here's how we're fixing it: [action]. Thanks for the honest feedback."

---

**4. Low Engagement / Poor Performance**

**Response:**
- Don't panic (normal for first few hours)
- Review messaging (is value prop clear?)
- Check traffic sources (are we reaching right audience?)
- Test different headlines/copy
- Reach out to warm contacts directly
- Adjust expectations and iterate

---

### Escalation Matrix

| Issue Severity | Response Time | Who Handles | Escalate To |
|----------------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Critical** (site down, security breach) | Immediate | Engineering + PM | CTO, CEO |
| **Major** (broken key feature, payment issues) | <15 min | PM + Eng | VP Product |
| **Medium** (high support volume, messaging confusion) | <1 hour | PMM + CS | Dir Marketing |
| **Low** (minor bugs, copy errors) | <4 hours | Assigned owner | No escalation |

## Post-Launch Day (Evening)

### End of Day Debrief (6:00pm)

**Meeting:** Core launch team (30 minutes)

**Agenda:**
1. **Metrics review:** Did we hit targets? What beat/missed expectations?
2. **Wins:** What went better than expected?
3. **Issues:** What problems did we face? How did we handle them?
4. **Learnings:** What would we do differently?
5. **Tomorrow:** What's the plan for Day 2?

---

### Team Celebration

**Important:** Take time to celebrate, even if results were mixed.

- Order team dinner
- Share wins publicly (thank team on social)
- Personal thank-yous to key contributors
- Take a breath before Day 2

---
